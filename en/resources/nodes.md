# RPC / Nodes (recommended + buy)

RPC directly affects minting speed & success. Start free, and get a paid dedicated RPC once competitive mints become frequent.

## Free (to start)

* **Chainlist** — [chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org)
  Search your chain, copy public RPC addresses → paste into the Nogada [RPC] screen. Keep only the low-ping ones.

## Paid (strongly recommended for competitive mints)

| Provider | Model | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Alchemy** — [alchemy.com](https://www.alchemy.com) | Pay-as-you-go (add card) | ~$1–5/mo for 50 wallets. Many RPC URLs per account. Easiest. |
| **DRPC** — [drpc.org](https://drpc.org) | Credit top-up | No rate limits. Small top-up lasts long. Many URLs per account. |
| **Ankr** — [ankr.com](https://www.ankr.com) | Credit top-up | Large infra. |
| **QuickNode** — [quicknode.com](https://www.quicknode.com) | Monthly subscription | Stable. |
| **Infura** — [infura.io](https://www.infura.io) | Free/paid | Ethereum standard. |

## Tips

* **Different providers work better on different chains.** Keep 1–3 ready and pick the fastest via **Test** on the [RPC] screen.
* The lower the supply / more FCFS the mint, the more RPC speed & stability matter — paid dedicated RPC wins on those days.
* No 100% answer. Your own tested experience is the best guide.

> ⚠️ Links/pricing change often. If a link is down, search the provider name.
